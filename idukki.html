Idukki

The district, which lies in the Western Ghats of Kerala, is topographically known for its rocky terrain and varied verdant vegetation. Places in Idukki is full of cliffs, lush greenery, lagoons, tranquil rivers and cascading waterfalls that hypnotize the tourists and leave them with everlasting memories. A proud home of South India's highest peak, Anamudi that stands at a height of 2,695 meters, Idukki is one of the most stunning hill stations in Kerala. ...
